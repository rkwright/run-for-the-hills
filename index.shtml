Moraine Hills Run for the Hills

10K and Half Marathon

Saturday, October 7, 2017

Moraine Hills State Park
1510 S. River Road

Click here for a Google map

Run in the peaceful autumn beauty of Northern Illinois’ premier Moraine Hills State Park. The courses are set completely within the park on asphalt and crushed limestone trails (no traffic!). Expect rolling hills and spectacular scenery. This is a race you shouldn’t miss.

8:30 a.m.for both distances (the race will be starting in a wave format)

NOTE: Course support (water stations and course marshals) will end at 11:45 a.m. Runners still on the course after this time will be notified.

All runners receive a finisher medal, Moraine Hills Run for the Hills shirt, and post-race nutrition. Place medals and prizes go to the top three M/F winners in each distance. Medals also go to the top three M/F winners in each age group: 19 and under, 20 – 24, 25 – 29, 30 – 34, 35 – 39, 40 – 44, 45 – 49, 50 – 54, 55 – 59, 60+.

Water and Gatorade stations are at approximately every 2 miles and the finish.

Sponsored by the Friends of Moraine Hills State Park, all proceeds from this fundraiser will benefit the education programs and visitor services at Moraine Hills State Park. The Friends have also set up an annual scholarship program for students of environmental studies.

Please visit the Friends of Moraine State Park for more information about the race and the park itself! Look forward to seeing you there, Saturday, October 7th!
