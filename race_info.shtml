2017 Race Info

The race will be entirely in the State Park on the combined asphalt and crushed lime trails (gorgeous!).

10K Course

10K = 6.2 miles on the Lake Defiance and Bog Trails.

Click on the map to see a larger version


Half-Marathon Course

The 10K and half-marathon runners will run the same course for the first 6 miles, then the half-marathoners run out to complete ONE loop of the McHenry Dam trail, an out and back on the River Road bike trail, and on to finish at Northern Woods

As always, there will be abundant signage, a leader bike for the the whole 13.1 miles and knowledgeable course marshals to direct you at all key intersections and turns.

There will be a brief course talk at 8 AM on Race Day at the Northern Woods site shelter.

If you have any questions, please contact the Race Director.
